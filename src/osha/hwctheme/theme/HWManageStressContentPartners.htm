Official European Campaign partners

We help create safer and healthier workplaces for everyone’s benefit, but we cannot do this by ourselves. For this reason, our Healthy Workplaces Campaign 2012–13 ‘Working together for risk prevention’ relies on a diverse range of partners and stakeholders, including governments and their agencies, employers, workers and businesses large and small.

Since its official launch on 18 April 2012 more than 80 companies and associations from the private and public sector, including some of Europe’s most well-known companies, have joined forces with the Agency to encourage managers, workers, representatives and other stakeholders to work together to manage risks in the workplaces.
