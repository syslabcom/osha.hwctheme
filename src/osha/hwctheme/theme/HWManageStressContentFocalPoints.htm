Focal Points

The Healthy Workplaces Campaign relies on the active support and collaboration of the Agency’s network of national ‘focal points’. These are typically the lead safety and health organisations in their respective countries, such as ministries, labour inspectorates or OSH research institutes.

The focal points support our initiatives with information and feedback and work with national networks including government and workers’ and employers’ representatives. The focal points coordinate the Healthy Workplaces Campaign at the national level, organise events and nominate representatives to our expert groups.

If you want to get in touch with your national focal point, click on the corrisponding focal point profile below.
